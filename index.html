  • Introduction

    We started the server because we wanted to relax from high population servers. We got tired of hackers and cheaters ruining our experience. We reported them, but it would take up to 48 hours for a response. So we set out to create a community that would make player experience a priority and not player population.

  • background

Cheaters and Toxic Players

We have zero tolerance to hackers, cheaters, and people who DDOS. Please be respectful and mature to all players on the server. We want our community to be welcoming to all players. This means no racism, homophobia, sexism, and transphobia to everyone. Spamming is also not allowed. People with VAC banned of less than 50 days or on 3 other accounts are not permitted onto the servers.

Wipes

Wipes happen on every Saturday at 3:00PM EST. In some cases unexpected wipes may happend. If this happens please know that this was for either a good reason or an unexpected occurance happened on the server(s).

Hope you enjoy!
